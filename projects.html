My Projects

Three years ago, SheCodes sparked my curiosity, but my journey had to wait as I immersed myself in Python and SQL for consulting. With a background in art history and architecture, I knew a creative spark was missing in my daily life. Rediscovering SheCodes felt like finding a long-lost passion, and diving into the world of coding just made sense. Take a look around, and feel free to get inspired!

My first project was full of pastel colors that feel like home, and it was so special to have Rey and Chewie cheering me on (from their naps). It’s amazing how these colors have become a theme in my work, and it all started here!

Ticking Around the Globe

The World Clock project was a thrilling exploration of JavaScript Date Formats and Time Zones! As we dove into the course, I was captivated by how we can manipulate time in so many ways. Understanding the intricacies of time zones opened up a whole new world for me, enabling me to create a functional clock that connects different parts of the globe.
